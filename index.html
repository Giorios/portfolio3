About me:

I enjoy the process of envisioning the design elements of a website, along with planning the appearance and position of the elements, and realizing those ideas into a website by creating the code that will bring the envisioned site to life.

I have a strong desire to pursue a career in the field of web design, specifically in roles that involve creating visually appealing and functional websites for a diverse range of clients.

My education consists of two years of Honors Web Design classes at Freehold High School. I am currently in my third and final year. I am very interested in furthering my web design education and gaining experience with designing webpages for clients after I graduate high school.
